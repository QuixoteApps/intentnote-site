Privacy Policy

Effective date:

Intent Note respects your privacy. We collect only what’s needed to provide the app, and we never sell your data.

Data we handle

  • Notes and Intentions: Stored on your device and synced with iCloud if enabled. We do not run our own servers for your content.
  • Calendar Access: With your permission, the app reads events to show your agenda and schedule local reminders. Event data stays on device.
  • Notifications: The app schedules local notifications you configure (e.g., daily summary). No third‑party push providers are used.
  • Purchases: Apple handles purchases and subscriptions via StoreKit. We receive non‑identifying purchase status to unlock features.
  • Analytics: If enabled in the future, analytics will be privacy‑respecting and minimal. We will update this page before enabling.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share personal data with third parties, except Apple services you opt into (iCloud, App Store purchases).

Your choices

  • You can revoke Calendar and Notification permissions in iOS Settings.
  • You can delete local data by removing the app from your device. iCloud data can be managed in iCloud Settings.

Children

Intent Note is not directed to children under 13.

Contact

Email: support@intentnote.app

Changes

We may update this policy. We will post updates here and revise the effective date.
